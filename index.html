Scale locked
Scale is fixed at 0.001, so Fusion millimetres always convert to metres for true 1:1 VR/AR. It cannot be changed by the UI or hand gestures.
Fusion / CAD Z-up fix
Leave this on if the model appears lying down without it.
Longest axis vertical
Normally on. Automatically rotates the model so its longest dimension becomes the vertical Z/up-down axis.
Centre model + floor align
Places the model in the middle of the grid with its lowest point on the floor.
Small model viewing height
For a 30 cm model, use floor mode for true-size placement, or raise it for close inspection outside XR.
Show metre grid
Each large grid square is 1 metre.
XR controllers / hand gestures
Controllers: left stick moves in VR or moves the OBJ horizontally in AR; right stick rolls/raises the OBJ. Hands: when Physical hand grab is off, right pinch-drag moves the OBJ and two-hand pinch can move/scale/twist. When Physical hand grab is on, normal pinch movement/scaling/roll gestures are disabled; the grab starts when the OBJ surface is between/under your fingers or palm and you close your hand, then releases when you open your hand. Two-hand pinch is still used for floor/ceiling calibration when your left fingertip is clearly in a calibration pose.
Physical hand grab
When this is on, normal pinch movement, pinch rolling, and two-hand pinch scaling/twisting are disabled. Put your fingers/palm onto or slightly through the OBJ surface, close your hand like you are picking up a real object, then open your hand to let go. It checks for mesh-surface contact or the surface being between your palm and fingers.
Show controllers / hands in XR
Shows controller models/rays and WebXR hand models attached to the same XR rig as the headset, so they stay aligned with real tracking.
Right-stick spin axis
Use Auto for rockets. It picks the model’s longest local axis. If it spins wrong, try X, Y, or Z.
Passthrough / AR mode
Use the Enter Passthrough button. The opaque floor and grid are hidden automatically so your real room shows behind the model.
AR floor placement
In passthrough, keep the bottom of the model on the calibrated floor. No head-height value is needed.
XR floor calibration
If the floor is wrong in VR/AR, put the left controller on the real floor, then press/hold A on the right controller and press the right trigger. With hand tracking, touch the floor with your left index fingertip, then pinch both hands together. In Auto mode, two-hand pinch away from the floor now transforms the OBJ instead of accidentally setting a cut. You can also nudge the floor up/down. In VR this is the only cut/height calibration.
AR clipping cuts
AR/passthrough only. Off until an AR ceiling cut is set. After setting the ceiling in passthrough, press left controller X to toggle clipping on/off.
A + right trigger / hand-pinch mode
Use Auto normally. Controllers use A + right trigger. Hand tracking uses both-hand pinch with the left index fingertip at the floor or clearly above eye level for Auto calibration. For a lower AR ceiling/cut height, choose Set AR ceiling cut first so it is not confused with two-hand move/scale/twist.
AR ceiling cut adjustment
Fine-tune the passthrough/AR ceiling clipping plane if the controller point is slightly off. This is disabled in VR.

Touch/drag to rotate outside VR. Pinch or wheel to zoom. Use Enter VR for a virtual room, or Enter Passthrough for Quest AR/passthrough. Controllers still work as before: in VR, the left stick moves you around; in AR/passthrough, the left stick moves the OBJ horizontally so real-world tracking stays stable. Right stick rolls the rocket around its engine-to-nose-cone axis, right stick up/down adjusts model height, and right-stick click resets only the OBJ height back to the calibrated floor. Hand tracking is now supported too: with Physical hand grab off, right pinch-drag moves the OBJ, left pinch-drag rolls it around the length axis, quick left pinch reveals markers, quick right pinch resets OBJ height, and two-hand pinch lets you move/twist the OBJ while scale stays locked at 0.001. With Physical hand grab on, normal pinch movement/scaling/roll gestures are disabled: put your fingers/palm onto or slightly through the OBJ surface, close your hand to pick it up, then open your hand to let it go. To set the floor or AR ceiling with hands, put the left index fingertip at the calibration height and pinch both hands together. Controller and hand visuals are attached to the headset XR rig, not the OBJ. Left controller X toggles AR cut-off clipping on/off after an AR ceiling is set. No controller-mounted UI panel is included.
