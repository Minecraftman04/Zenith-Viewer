Normally on. Automatically rotates the model so its longest dimension becomes the vertical Z/up-down axis.
Centre model + floor align
Places the model in the middle of the grid with its lowest point on the floor.
Small model viewing height
For a 30 cm model, use floor mode for true-size placement, or raise it for close inspection outside XR.
Show metre grid
Each large grid square is 1 metre.
XR controller sticks
Left thumbstick moves you around. Right thumbstick left/right rolls the model. Right thumbstick up/down moves the model height. Click the right stick to reset it to floor level.
Show controllers in XR
Shows controller models/rays attached to the same XR rig as the headset, so they stay aligned with your real controllers.
Right-stick spin axis
Use Auto for rockets. It picks the model’s longest local axis. If it spins wrong, try X, Y, or Z.
Passthrough / AR mode
Use the Enter Passthrough button. The opaque floor and grid are hidden automatically so your real room shows behind the model.
AR floor placement
In passthrough, keep the bottom of the model on the calibrated floor. No head-height value is needed.
XR floor calibration
If the floor is wrong in VR/AR, put the left controller on the real floor, then press the right trigger. You can also nudge the floor up/down.
XR clipping cuts
Off until a ceiling cut is set. After setting the ceiling, press left controller X to toggle clipping on/off in XR.
Right trigger clipping mode
Use Auto normally. Put the left controller on the floor or at the ceiling height, then press the right trigger.
Ceiling cut adjustment
Fine-tune the ceiling clipping plane if the controller point is slightly off.

Touch/drag to rotate outside VR. Pinch or wheel to zoom. Use Enter VR for a virtual room, or Enter Passthrough for Quest AR/passthrough. Left stick moves; right stick rolls the rocket around its engine-to-nose-cone axis, right stick up/down adjusts model height, and right-stick click resets it to the floor. Controller visuals are attached to the headset XR rig, not the OBJ. Right trigger confirms the left-controller floor/ceiling clipping cuts. Left controller X toggles cut-off clipping on/off after a ceiling is set. Left controller Y briefly fades the fixed floor/ceiling markers in, then fades them out again. No controller-mounted UI panel is included. AR startup is unchanged from the clean build.
